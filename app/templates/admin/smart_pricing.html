How It Works

For each product, you can enable a markup percentage. The customer always sees the adjusted price — there is no crossed-out base price shown.

Example: Base price 3,000 Birr + 3% = 3,090 Birr displayed to customer.

Products priced {{ "{:,.0f}".format(qty_min_price | float) }}+ Birr (after adjustment) count toward quantity discounts.
